Detective tale and cosmic opera collide in psychedelic adventure Nirvana Noir

In an exclusive interview with Gameloo, the creative team behind the upcoming psychedelic adventure game Nirvana Noir revealed how they're blending hardboiled detective fiction with cosmic horror elements to create a truly unique experience.

"We wanted to create something that feels both familiar and completely alien at the same time," explained lead designer Denis Abel. "The detective framework gives players something to hold onto while we take them on this wild journey through fractured realities."

The game features a revolutionary time-bending mechanic that allows players to experience scenes from multiple perspectives simultaneously. "It's not just about solving a mystery," added writer Evan Anthony. "It's about understanding how perspective changes truth itself."

Key Features:

  • Non-linear narrative with multiple endings
  • Psychedelic visual effects that evolve with gameplay
  • Original jazz-noir soundtrack with dynamic composition
  • Mind-bending puzzles that play with perception
  • Fully voiced characters with branching dialogues

Nirvana Noir is scheduled for release on Gameloo in Q2 2024, with a special demo available to newsletter subscribers next month.

Developed by:Surya Dahiya

Platforms: PC, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S

Release Date: June 15, 2025
