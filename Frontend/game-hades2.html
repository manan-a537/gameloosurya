About The Game

Hades 2 is the electrifying sequel to Supergiant Games' award-winning rogue-like dungeon crawler. As the immortal Princess of the Underworld, you'll explore a bigger, deeper mythic world, vanquishing the forces of the Titan of Time with the full might of Olympus behind you. The game features all-new weapons, abilities, and boons from the gods, along with the same signature gameplay that made the original a masterpiece. With its addictive combat, rich storytelling, and stunning hand-painted art style, Hades 2 builds upon everything that made the first game one of the most beloved indie titles of all time.
