About The Game

Brawlhalla is a free-to-play platform fighting game that brings together over 50 unique characters from across history and mythology. With simple controls perfect for beginners but deep mechanics for competitive play, Brawlhalla offers endless fun for players of all skill levels. Battle it out in multiple game modes including 1v1, 2v2, 4-player free-for-all, and 8-player mayhem. Featuring regular updates, cross-play across all platforms, and a thriving esports scene, Brawlhalla continues to grow with new legends, weapons, maps, and game modes. Whether you're playing casually with friends or competing at the highest level, Brawlhalla delivers fast-paced, action-packed combat.
