About The Game

Welcome to San Francisco. Play as Marcus Holloway, a brilliant young hacker living in the birthplace of the tech revolution, San Francisco Bay Area. Team up with Dedsec, a notorious group of hackers, to execute the biggest hack in history and take down ctOS 2.0, an invasive operating system being used by criminal masterminds to monitor and manipulate citizens on a massive scale. Watch Dogs 2 delivers a fresh open world experience with new hacking abilities, more weapons and vehicles, and greater freedom in how you choose to complete your missions.
