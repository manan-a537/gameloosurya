About The Game

Path of Exile is a free-to-play action RPG set in the dark fantasy world of Wraeclast. With its deep character customization, complex skill system, and endless itemization options, Path of Exile offers one of the most rewarding ARPG experiences available. Choose from seven character classes and embark on a journey through dark and dangerous landscapes, battling horrifying creatures and uncovering ancient secrets. With regular expansions and a passionate community, Path of Exile continues to evolve and grow, offering new challenges and adventures with each season.

The game features a massive passive skill tree with over 1,300 skills, a revolutionary skill gem system, and competitive PvP and racing modes. Play solo or team up with friends in the online multiplayer mode. With no pay-to-win elements and purely cosmetic microtransactions, Path of Exile remains true to the spirit of classic ARPGs.
