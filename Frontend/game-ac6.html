About The Game

Armored Core VI: Fires of Rubicon is a mecha-based vehicular combat game developed by FromSoftware. Set in a dystopian future where corporations battle over a mysterious substance called "Coral" on the planet Rubicon 3, players take on the role of a mercenary pilot controlling powerful customizable mechs known as Armored Cores.

The game combines FromSoftware's signature gameplay with fast-paced mech combat, deep customization systems, and intense boss battles. Players will assemble their perfect war machine from hundreds of parts and weapons, then test their builds in high-octane combat scenarios across devastated environments.

Key Features:

  • FromSoftware's first Armored Core game in 10 years
  • Fully customizable mechs with hundreds of parts
  • Fast-paced, technical mech combat
  • Massive boss battles against other ACs and war machines
  • Mission-based structure with branching paths
  • Stunning sci-fi visuals and destructible environments
  • Online PvP battles (1v1 and 3v3)
  • New assault boost system for aggressive combat
