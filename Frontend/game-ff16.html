About The Game

Final Fantasy XVI is the next mainline installment in the legendary Final Fantasy series, developed by Square Enix. Set in the dark fantasy world of Valisthea, the story follows Clive Rosfield, a young man sworn to protect his younger brother Joshua, the Dominant of the Phoenix. When a tragic event shatters Clive's world, he embarks on a quest for revenge that will shape the fate of the entire realm.

The game features a mature narrative, intense real-time combat, and breathtaking Eikon battles that redefine the franchise. Experience a rich story of political intrigue, personal drama, and world-shaking conflicts as Clive uncovers the dark secrets behind the Mothercrystals and the Blight that threatens to consume Valisthea.

Key Features:

  • Dark fantasy setting with mature themes
  • Revolutionary real-time combat system
  • Epic Eikon battles with cinematic scale
  • Deep character-driven narrative
  • Expansive world of Valisthea to explore
  • Stunning visuals powered by next-gen technology
  • DualSense controller haptic feedback
  • Original score by Masayoshi Soken
