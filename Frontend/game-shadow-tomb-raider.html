About The Game

Shadow of the Tomb Raider: Definitive Edition includes the base game, all seven DLC challenge tombs, as well as all downloadable weapons, outfits, and skills. This is the ultimate way to experience Lara Croft's defining moment as she becomes the Tomb Raider.

In Shadow of the Tomb Raider, Lara must master a deadly jungle, overcome terrifying tombs, and persevere through her darkest hour. As she races to save the world from a Maya apocalypse, Lara will ultimately be forged into the Tomb Raider she is destined to be.

Key Features:

  • Explore the most immersive Tomb Raider game yet with challenging puzzles and breathtaking environments
  • Experience high-octane action with new traversal options like rappelling and swimming
  • Play all seven DLC challenge tombs with additional puzzles to solve
  • Unlock new outfits, weapons, and skills to customize your playstyle
  • Definitive Edition includes 4K resolution with HDR and improved graphics

Live Lara's journey in its entirety with the Definitive Edition, featuring all previously released content in one complete package.
