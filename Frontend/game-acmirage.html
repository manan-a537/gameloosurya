About The Game

Assassin's Creed Mirage takes players back to the roots of the franchise with a focus on stealth, parkour, and assassination. Set in 9th-century Baghdad during the Islamic Golden Age, players take on the role of Basim Ibn Ishaq, a street thief who becomes entangled with the Hidden Ones. The game returns to the series' core gameplay elements, featuring a more compact and dense city environment perfect for parkour and stealth gameplay. With a renewed emphasis on social stealth, players must blend into crowds and use the environment to their advantage. The game's narrative explores Basim's journey from street thief to master assassin, while also delving into the rich history and culture of Baghdad during its golden age. Mirage offers a more focused and intimate experience compared to recent entries in the series, making it a perfect entry point for new players and a nostalgic return for longtime fans.
