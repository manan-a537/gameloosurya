About The Game

Mortal Kombat 1 is the explosive rebirth of the iconic fighting franchise from NetherRealm Studios. Featuring a fresh timeline created by Fire God Liu Kang, the game introduces reimagined versions of classic characters with all-new backstories and fatalities. Experience next-gen visuals with the most brutal combat system yet, including the revolutionary Kameo Fighter system that allows for unprecedented team-up attacks. With its cinematic story mode, online multiplayer, and deepest combat mechanics in series history, Mortal Kombat 1 delivers the ultimate fighting game experience with the signature gore and violence fans expect.
