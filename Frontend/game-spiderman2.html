About The Game

Marvel's Spider-Man 2 is the highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed Marvel's Spider-Man, developed by Insomniac Games. This installment brings together both Peter Parker and Miles Morales as they face new threats in an expanded New York City, including the terrifying symbiote Venom. The game continues the emotional, action-packed story of both Spider-Men as they balance their personal lives with their superhero responsibilities.

The game features enhanced web-swinging mechanics, new abilities for both Spider-Men, and a darker storyline as the symbiote takes hold. Experience faster traversal with the new web wings, intense combat with evolved abilities, and a visually stunning New York City that's nearly twice as large as the previous game's map, now including Brooklyn and Queens.

Key Features:

  • Play as both Peter Parker and Miles Morales
  • New symbiote abilities and evolved combat
  • Expanded New York City with new boroughs to explore
  • Faster traversal with web wings for gliding
  • Epic showdowns with classic villains including Venom
  • Deeper character development and emotional storytelling
  • Stunning visuals taking full advantage of PS5 hardware
  • DualSense controller haptic feedback and adaptive triggers
