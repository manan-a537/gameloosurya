About The Game

Grand Theft Auto V is a vast open-world game set in the fictional state of San Andreas, based on Southern California. The story follows three criminals and their efforts to commit heists while under pressure from a government agency. Players control all three protagonists throughout the single-player campaign, switching between them both during and outside missions. The game world is navigated on foot or by vehicle, from either a first-person or third-person perspective. With the addition of Grand Theft Auto Online, players can engage in cooperative and competitive multiplayer modes, constantly updated with new content. GTA V offers unprecedented freedom to explore, cause chaos, or follow an engaging narrative across one of the most detailed game worlds ever created.
