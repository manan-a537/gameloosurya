Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? +
To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.
How can I request a refund? +
You can request a refund within 14 days of purchase if you've played less than 2 hours. Go to your purchase history and select the game you want to refund.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and various local payment methods. Check the payment options during checkout for a complete list.
